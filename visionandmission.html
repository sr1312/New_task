Our Vision and Mission



Chegus Infotech is a young, dynamic and progressive thinking IT – PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT Company started in Bangalore, India and is a part of Chegus Group which is into commodity trading with operations in West Africa (Ghana, Ivory Coast, Burkina Faso & Bissau) for the past 9 years with an annual group turnover of about USD 30 million.

Our Vision and Mission is to cater to the requirements of INSURANCE COMPANIES

* With aggressive pricing.

* Low Capex for clients.

* Quick response during post sales SERVICE.

* Customizable to suit each client’s business model.

* Efficient data migration.

We work with our clients in a simple and highly effective manner to understand their requirements and to invent innovative ways to develop client organization and its performance and at the same time check on operational costs to assure sustained profitability.
