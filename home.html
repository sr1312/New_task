Chegus InfoTech is a product development oriented Software company focused on Core Insurance and Banking domains. Chegus is a privately owned Group which started its operations in 2008.

The Software development business was conceptualized in Nov 2012 and Chegus InfoTech was formed in March 2013 in the city of Bangalore, India . Currently, our focus is development of applications related to Insurance industry and our next target is to expand into financial products for small & medium scale financial institutions.
